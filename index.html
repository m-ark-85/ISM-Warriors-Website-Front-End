What do we do?

Our two main objectives are providing verified contacts according to the need and spreading awareness via social media. First of all, it's better if you go through the available covid resources we have and understand how you can respond quickly with proper and verified information. All the information and contacts must be verified recently before providing to the needy one. Also try to gather more and more recently updated resources. Also everyone can put status on Whatsapp,story in instagram and tweets for needy ones to dm you so that we can help more and more.
